Our story

The family we've created together.

Some of life's best moments aren't planned.

What started as two people finding each other became something much bigger — a family. Over the years, we have built a life together filled with love, laughter, adventures, everyday moments and memories that mean the world to us.

Aubrey is such a special part of our story, and this wedding is not only about Brendan and Linley. It is about celebrating the family we have become, the people who have supported us, and the future we are building together.

On 13 March 2027, surrounded by the people who mean the most to us, we will be making it official. We cannot wait to share the day with you.

We will confirm this directly with families once the final guest list is locked in.

What should I wear?

Cocktail / formal. The setting is rooftop and riverside, so dress for a beautiful evening out.

Yes. We are planning a bell ringing demonstration with an opportunity for guests to ring the bells themselves.

Yes, fireworks are planned over the Swan River as part of the celebration.
